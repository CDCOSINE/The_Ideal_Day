Strategize priority for next week

Strategization includes following operations:

In general task which you tend to complete most of times goes down in priority, task which you find harder to complete will become more important.

1. Tasks completed 7 times will be assigned minimum priority

2. Tasks completed 0 times will be assigned maximum priority

3. Priority of Tasks completed less than 3 times (more than 0) will be doubled i.e. priority[task] = 2*priroty[task]

4. Priority of Tasks completed more than 3 times (less than 7) will be doubled i.e. priority[task] = priroty[task]/2

Note: Strategy button is only available on Sunday after 7pm after showing previous week's score.
